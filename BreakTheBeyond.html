    Swap Mechanic

    As the game is not a deck type card game, attaining cards and consuming them is very important. Many of games players wanted to discard cards to make space to the hand. This would have changed the game completely, taking the game into direction the team did not wish to take it.

    Swap allowed meaningful decision making, without causing damage to the difficulty curve of the game. Each loot card have an indicator showing it can be switched between loot pile and hand.

    Mechanic not only gave player choice to affect their hand, but also gave better user experience overall.
